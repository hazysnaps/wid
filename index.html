About Us

Our Story

The Widaad Project is a social enterprise that runs as a non-profit organisation of which all of our profits will be channeled into our community projects and the costs of running the enterprise. We are made up of a group of youths with diverse backgrounds who have come together to organise community-centred events in a bid to do our part in the society. Our goal is to make a difference, and at the same time gain new skills through experience and the people we meet. We hope to uplift and bring betterment to our chosen beneficiaries, and to initiate change in their lives so that they can strive to be better than the circumstances that they are in.
A local project where we mentor a group of youth-at-risk. The year-long mentorship will focus on character building and equipping the youth with lifelong skills. We hope to boost their confidence and self-esteem in their own varied abilities. An overseas project where we render help to a chosen village and their local school in Cambodia. We will carry out refurbishment works, upgrading and adding of essential facilities, and conduct lessons of subject that aren't offered in the local school.
